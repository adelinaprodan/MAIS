Deep in the Black Forest

by Irsa

Living in Furtwangen, especially in the GHB rooms, is inevitable to not deal with the black forest view.Calling all students of Furtwangen! The Black Forest, right at your doorstep, is not just a scenic wonder but an unparalleled outdoor classroom awaiting exploration.

Why not to get together and explore what's around you?The Black Forest offers more than stunning landscapes; it's a natural playground for team-building activities and outdoor adventures. Strengthen camaraderie with your fellow students through hiking, exploring, and overcoming challenges together. These shared experiences foster a sense of unity and create lasting memories.

In the welcoming embrace of good weather, the Black Forest transforms into the perfect setting for memorable outings. Grab your picnic basket, gather your friends, and relish the opportunity to bask in the sunshine amidst the lush greenery.

Discover the Black Forest's charm year-round: Picnics and fireside gatherings in good weather, and the magical spectacle of snow-covered trees in winter. Whether enjoying a sunny picnic or embracing the winter wonderland, Furtwangen's Black Forest offers diverse and enchanting experiences throughout the seasons.

Location? We all know that you do not need a location to access black forest. It is all around us but not only. Check the map to see our huge “Schwarzwald”.

⚠ But be careful, there is no internet access in the most part of the forest so be sure not to go alone and not to get lost inside of it, as some of our friends did 😂.
